How it works ▾
  1. Draft six golfers. Pick two from each tier. Tiers are set automatically by ranking, so no one argues over who's a favourite.
  2. Best four count. Your four best scores to par are added up; the worst two are dropped, so a missed cut won't sink you.
  3. Lowest total wins. The lowest combined score to par takes the pool. Ties broken by the best single golfer's finish.
  4. Picks stay hidden until the first tee shot, then everyone's entry reveals and the standings go live.
  5. The pot. $20 buy-in from everyone. Winner takes 70%, runner-up takes 30%.
